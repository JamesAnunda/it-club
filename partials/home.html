Information Technology Club

ABOUT

The School of Computing & Informatics of Ivy Tech Community College Northeast, would like to introduce you to our brand new club!

The IT Club engages members through talks and presentations by local practitioners, visits and discussions from prominent speakers, technical and career workshops, field trips to local industries and regional tours, informative and fundraising computing fairs, and social activities including WAN/LAN parties and movies. Our club will be participating in official competitions and challenges in various computing and informatics areas.

We meet on the Coliseum Campus in Room 2312. Events and times listed below.

Community & Student Engagement

EVENTS

The IT Club holds student-led scientific, educational and casual events dedicated to advancing the arts, sciences, and applications of information technology, in a fun, upbeat, casual way. Students and community members are encouraged to attend all events and bring friends to have a fun and infomative time learning and growing in various areas of computing and informatics.

Remember to RSVP! Bring a friend, let's have some fun!
