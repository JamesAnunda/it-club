What Members Can Expect:

As a department organization, we are committed to to demystifing code and new technology to broaden participation in the field of computer science!

We partner with FreeCodeCamp, a non-profit organization that consists of an interactive learning web platform, an online community forum, chat rooms, and Medium publications, intending to make learning web development accessible to anyone.

Through our events, workshops, technology and partnerships, we facilitate students' growth and enrich their education. Students can expect to learn the basics of these technologies:

  • HTML5
  • CSS3
  • Bootstrap 4
  • JavaScript
  • jQuery
  • ...and more
